Methods Lunch

Research Roundtable: Healthy Brain Network Dataset

Join Jessica M. Lammert, PhD Candidate in the Neuroplasticity in Sensory Systems Lab, as she introduces the Healthy Brain Network (HBN)—a large-scale open-access dataset for developmental neuroscience research. Learn how to access this dataset on the CBS server and gain practical tips for working with “Big Data” in neuroscience. In the second half, Jessica will share her ongoing analyses of HBN data and invite feedback in our pilot Research Roundtable format—a collaborative space to discuss ideas, tackle challenges, and strengthen research.

Jessica M. Lammert
